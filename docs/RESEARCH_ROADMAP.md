1. Reproduce toy scaling, cutoff, mixture, recursion, and verification mechanisms.
2. Build a controlled MMALS route-tail benchmark.
3. Test recursive route collapse and real-anchor recovery.
4. Test best-of-K route generation with an independent verifier.

6. Add probabilistic MTP source effects and effective evidence size.
7. Add geometric state/uncertainty only where matched Euclidean baselines fail.
8. Qualify claims with causal ablations, cross-seed replication, and deployment cost.
